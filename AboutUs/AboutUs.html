About Us

Central Electro Traders

Central Electro Traders, established in 1960 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, has emerged as a reputable Sole Proprietorship firm in the industrial sector. We specialize in wholesale trading of Phase Failure Relays, Voltage Monitoring Relays, Monitoring Relays, and various other industrial equipment. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice among clients.

Our products are renowned for their premium quality, seamless finish, and diverse patterns, making them highly sought after in the market. We prioritize affordability without compromising on quality, ensuring that our offerings meet the needs of a wide range of customers.

With years of experience and a strong business ethic, we continue to uphold our reputation for excellence in the industry.s we look toward the future, we remain dedicated to serving our clients with the same level of passion and commitment that has defined us thus far. With a focus on continuous improvement and innovation, we eagerly anticipate the opportunity to exceed expectations and continue our journey of excellence for many years to come..
